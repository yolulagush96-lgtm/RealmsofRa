About Us

Realms of Ra opens its doors on 2 June 2026 in the bohemian suburb of Melville, Johannesburg, in sunny South Africa.

A Shared journey

Our mission is to make alternative spirituality and genuine guidance beautifully affordable and accessible to all, ensuring that the esoteric world remains an open garden rather than an exclusive space. There is no marketing fluff here—just a thoughtfully selected, authentic collection of crystals, premium candles, incense, and tarot decks to support your journey.

While Protea Phoenix offers accessible guidance across the airwaves, Realms of Ra serves as the physical, brick-and-mortar anchor. Your support does so much more than keep our doors open; it helps us create a space for community, healing, and the preservation of the old ways. Together, we stand for a collective rising from the ashes—a reclamation of personal peace, a return to the sacredness of the Earth, and a warm, open door for anyone ready to walk the old paths in a modern world.
